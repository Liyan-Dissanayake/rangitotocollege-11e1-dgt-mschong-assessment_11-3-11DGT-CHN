Must-Visit Attractions in Sri Lanka

Discover the most breathtaking places that make Sri Lanka a paradise island

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ancient rock fortress and palace ruins with famous frescoes and water gardens, built by King Kashyapa in the 5th century.

Nine Arch Bridge, Ella

Iconic railway bridge surrounded by lush tea plantations, offering spectacular views and photo opportunities.

Yala National Park

Sri Lanka's most famous wildlife sanctuary, home to leopards, elephants, and diverse bird species.

Temple of the Sacred Tooth

UNESCO World Heritage Site in Kandy housing Buddha's tooth relic, an important pilgrimage site.

Galle Dutch Fort

Historical fort built by the Portuguese and Dutch, featuring colonial architecture and charming streets.

Adam's Peak

Sacred mountain with the famous 'Sri Pada' footprint, offering breathtaking sunrise views from the summit.

Mirissa Whale Watching

One of the best places in the world to see blue whales and dolphins in their natural habitat.

Nuwara Eliya Tea Plantations

Rolling hills covered in vibrant green tea bushes, with opportunities for factory tours and tastings.

Sacred City of Anuradhapura

One of the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, famous for its well-preserved ruins of ancient Lankan civilization and sacred Buddhist sites dating back to 4th century BC.
